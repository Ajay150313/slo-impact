Uptime Explained

User-Impact Uptime weights each incident by the fraction of users actually affected and the traffic volume at the time — not just raw availability.

user_impact_uptime = 1 − (
  Σ duration × user_fraction × traffic_weight
) / window_seconds

// A 3 AM outage at 5% traffic counts far
// less than a noon outage at peak load.

SLO targets are evaluated against user-impact uptime, not raw availability. See slo-impact on GitHub for full methodology.
